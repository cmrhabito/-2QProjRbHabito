Know more about Red Velvet's (Korean) Discography

Their Korean discogrpahy consists of 2 full albums, 2 repackages/compilations, 2 predebut singles, and 9 mini albums. (list from KProfiles, covers from Wikipedia)

Queendom

latest release!

Red Velvet's most recent comeback after a hiatus of almost two years is mini album Queendom with a title track of the same name. It was released on August 17, 2021 and consists of six tracks. The comeback has 7 music show wins as of January 2022. Add a valid stream by watching through the embedded link (via SMTOWN youtube channel)!
