Joy, Wendy, and Yeri

Wendy

Instagram Source Image

Wendy (Son Seung-Wan) was born on February 21, 1994 in Seoul and moved to USA and later Canada with her older sister at 12, moving back to Korea later in 2012 to become the main vocalist of Red Velvet.

Like Water

The release of mini album Like Water on April 5, 2021 marked the first official solo debut among the members. The 5th and last track in the album called Best Friend featured member SEULGI.

Yeri

Instagram Source Image

Kim Ye-rim is the youngest member of Red Velvet, born on March 5, 1999. She was the last to join the group's lineup during Ice Cream Cake when she was 16 years old, as a sub vocalist and sub rapper.

Blue Birthday

Yeri is the main lead, along with Pentagon Hongseok, in web drama and movie of the same name Blue Birthday. The drama was premiered on July 23 and the movie was officially released on January 19, 2021.

Joy

Instagram Source Image

Joy (Park Soo-young) is a lead rapper, sub vocalist, and visual born on September 3, 1996. She made her official acting debut in 2017 when she played the lead role as Yoon So-Rim in web drama The Liar and His Lover.

HELLO

Joy is the second to debut as a solo artist in the group with mini album HELLO. The EP is a special album comprised of 6 covers of songs released in the 1990s, the title track originally sung by Park Hye-Kyung.
