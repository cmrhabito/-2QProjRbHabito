Achievements

Throughout the 7 years of their career, Red Velvet has been a significant figure in the international spread of K-pop, even going so far as to be the first K-pop group to perform in North Korea in 2018 as part of a special concert aired by MBC, though without Joy. They are one of the most successful and recognizable K-pop girl groups to date with 80 wins and 14 bonsangs. 10 of their music videos have surpassed 100 million views on YouTube and two are already in the range of 90 million. Read on to know more about their specific achievements throughout the years! (source)

Awards

They successfully won the most awards in 2018 and 2019 with 11 awards for both years. They have been nominated the most in Mnet Asian Music Awards but have won most often in Soribada. (source 1, source 2, image)

Charts and Sales

The group achieved their first Perfect All-Kill with "Power Up" from album "Summer Magic" following its release in August 2018, arguably their most successful release, holding the title of most sold album of the group until June 2019, on top of the comeback having the highest number of music show wins. As of January 2022, they have almost 2 million Korean sales and almost 50 thousand sales in their Japanese discography. Peek-a-Boo and Russian Roulette both peaked at #2 on the Billboard World Digital Songs chart. They are the first K-pop girl group to top the Billboard World Albums chart four times. (source)
