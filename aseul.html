IRENE&SEULGI

Monster

Monster by IRENE&SEULGI was released on July 7, 2020 marking the official debut of the group's first subunit. The music video reached 11.4M views and 1.7M likes in the first 24 hours after release. A choreography video for Naughty was also released on July 20, 2020 and was promoted by the pair after Monster on music shows. (via SMTOWN youtube channel)!

Subunit Activities

Red Velvet debuted two eldest members Irene and Seulgi as a subunit during the hiatus of Wendy from group activities, which started in late 2019, due to an injury. The official date was announced on June 21, 2020, although the original plans to release the album on June 15 were moved to July 6 due to extra time needed to complete the album. The title track peaked at #2 on Gaon chart in South Korea and got its first win on July 17, 2020 with 6,467 points. The music video reached 100 million views on June 4, 2021. (source 1, source 2)

Irene

Irene (Bae Joo-hyun) is the leader and eldest member of the group, born on March 29, 1991. Her positions are main rapper, lead dancer, sub vocalist, visual, and center. She trained for 5 years starting in 2009. She debuted as an actor as lead in movie Double Patty. Source Image

Seulgi

Kang Seulgi is the group's main dancer and vocalist and was born on February 10, 1994. She has collaborated with many other artists including NCT Taeyong and GOT7 Bambam. She is a member of SM's project girl group GIRLS ON TOP. Source Image
