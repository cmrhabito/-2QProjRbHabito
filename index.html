Red Velvet

is a South Korean 5-member girl group managed by SM Entertainment. They debuted with single "Happiness" with four members Irene, Seulgi, Wendy, and Joy on August 1, 2014 and Yeri joined in March 2015 in the group's first mini album Ice Cream Cake. The group's first subunit IRENE&SEULGI debuted on July 6, 2020 with EP Monster while members Wendy and Joy released their solo debut albums "Like Water" and "Hello" respectively in 2021.
